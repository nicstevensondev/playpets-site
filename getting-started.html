Getting Started

A quick overview of how to install, pin, and use your PlayPets cat. You can refine the details here later—this is just the structure.

  1. 1. Install the extension

    Visit the Chrome Web Store page for PlayPets and click Add to Chrome. Confirm the permissions when prompted.

  2. 2. Pin the extension

    Open the Chrome toolbar menu, locate PlayPets, and click the pin icon so it’s always available in your browser.

  3. 3. Meet your cat

    After installation, the cat appears along the edge of your browser window. You can drag it to a comfortable spot and start interacting.

  4. 4. Try different modes

    Use the extension popup to toggle play behavior, study behavior, or other options you add later. The cat will react accordingly.

  5. 5. Learn more

    You can update this page with short GIFs, screenshots, or advanced tips once the extension UI is finalized.

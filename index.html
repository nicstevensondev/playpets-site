What PlayPets Can Do

The extension is built to be charming but unobtrusive, with subtle interactions that match your focus level.

Playful when you want

Enable play mode to let your cat chase yarn, react to your mouse, and add a bit of warmth while you browse.

Calm while you focus

Switch to study behavior so your companion stays relaxed and subtle while you work or read.

Simple, transparent privacy

PlayPets runs locally in your browser. No logins, no tracking, and a clear privacy policy you can read in one sitting.

Cat Settings and Control Panel

Pin the extension and click the PlayPets icon to access your control panel. Here, you can change the name, visibility, and size of the cat.

Ready to adopt your browser companion?

Install the extension, pin it to your toolbar, and you’ll meet your cat in just a few clicks.
